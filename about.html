About Us

Pegasus Restaurant is located in Center Harbor at 23 Main Street, across from the center harbor town beach. the restaurant features a casual relaxed dining atmosphere with a lake front feel. Pegasus Restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as a full bar. The breakfast menu features pancakes made from scratch, fluffy omelets, waffles with fruit salsa and fresh roasted Mad River coffee. The lunch menu serves up creative salads, wraps, sandwiches & fresh burgers.
